# Day 3 - Prompt Engineering

## What is Prompt Engineering?
Ans- It is the process of writting clear and effectivre instructions in order to get the best results from an AI Model.

## What is a System Prompt?
Ans- Its an instruction that tells AI how it should behave throughout the conversation.

## What is a User Prompt?
Ans- Its the instructions or questions given by user to AI.

## What is a Persona?
Ans- Its basically the role assigned to the AI that affects how it responds.
eg.DSA Mentor
Career Coach
Networking Professor
GenAI Expert

## Why do System Prompts Matter?
Ans-It controls AI's behavior, tone and style making it more useful and accurate for the specific tasks.
